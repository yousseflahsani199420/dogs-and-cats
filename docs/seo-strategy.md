# PetZone SEO Strategy

## Title selection

- Prioritize long-tail keywords first for easier ranking wins.
- Keep titles clear, readable, and problem-oriented.
- Use the primary keyword once near the beginning of the title.

## Keyword usage guide

- Title: place the target keyword early without making the title robotic.
- H1: usually mirror the title closely.
- H2s: break the topic into intent-driven sections such as symptoms, routine, mistakes, and next steps.
- Internal links: connect every article to 3 or more related posts in the same cluster.

## Topical authority plan

- Publish clusters across cats and dogs instead of isolated one-off articles.
- Build around food, training, behavior, health, and beginner care.
- Use tag pages and category hubs to reinforce keyword relationships.

## Scaling from 1 to 300 articles

- Keep the file-based post schema stable.
- Let the queue prioritize low-competition long-tail opportunities first.
- Expand from seed articles to daily automation without changing the frontend.
- Review Search Console data monthly and feed new winning keywords back into `data/keywords.json`.
